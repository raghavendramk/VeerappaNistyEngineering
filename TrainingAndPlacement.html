VNEC Shorapur has a focused Placement Cell. We strongly believe that Students and recruiting organizations are two of our most important customers. The Placement Cell of the Institute is responsible for arranging training of the undergraduate and postgraduate students in order to make them comfortable in the recruitment process of various organizations and to provide world class job opportunities in a range of private and public sector organizations. The Placement Cell also arranges pre-placement orientation to the students prior to formal campus interviews from senior executives of major industries. VNEC conducts extensive on-campus recruitment programs, workshops, seminars and individual career and job search assistance. VNEC has a close interaction with organizations such as TCS, Wipro, IBM, Marine etc. for research, academic and Training & Placement activities. Campus interviews for graduate /postgraduate students normally commence in VII semester for B.E graduates, III semester for MBA and M.Tech.

Training:- The Institute takes care to train students for the campus recruitment. A structured Training programme for both IT and Non-IT branches students is put in place. VNEC regularly conducts training on topics such as Quantitative ability, Technical ability, Verbal ability, Logical ability, Group discussion, personal interview and communication skills with the assistance of best trainers. VNEC encourages interdisciplinary approach and thus exposes Non IT branch students to software languages like C, C++ , JAVA , PYTHON ,C#,.NET etc. VNEC organizes Industrial tours to give students a feel of actual work culture
