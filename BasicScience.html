About Departments

DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

Department of Mathematics has been established since 2010. Offers Engg. Mathematics I, Engg. Mathematics -II, Engg. Mathematics - III, Engg. Mathematics IV, Discrete mathematical Structures, Graph theory for B. E students


DEPARTMENT OF CHEMISTRY

Department of Chemistry established since the inception of the institution. The Department consists of well qualified and experienced faculty/staff working towards objective of the institution. The Department aims at providing sound knowledge in fundamental subjects which is essential for all Engineering students. The faculty members are committed towards career growth, institutional progress and are actively engaged in perusing research through sustained effort. The teaching staffs of the Department have published many papers in national and international journals. The major area of research is nano science, medicinal chemistry, membrane technology and natural products


DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICS

The Department of Physics has come into existence since the inception of the Institute in 2001. Engineering Physics is common for First Year Students of all the branches. The department is involved in teaching the Engineering Physics Theory and Laboratory courses to the students of different Engineering disciplines. The department of Physics has 9 well qualified and experienced faculty members. The Physics department has very good infrastructure with a working area of 392 sq mtr consisting of spacious laboratory, dark room, staff room, departmental library.


DEPARTMENT OF HUMANITIES

Courses like Environment Sciences, Constitution of India, Ethics and Technical Communications is conducted under the aegis of the Humanities Department.
