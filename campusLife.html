Facilities

1.HOSTEL FACILITIES

The College has modern hostel facilities in the campus for both boys and girls with in-house mess facility providing hygienic and nutritious diet. Safety and comfort of the students is the primary focus and in-house wardens monitor the welfare of the students. The hostels have solar systems and electrical geyser s for providing hot water apart from the modern amenities like recreation facilities, library facility etc.

At present the college has one boy's hostel blocks with a total of 250 capacities and one girl's hostel with a capacity of 150.
The college provides Excellent Hostel Facilities for Boys and Girls with built up area of 3500 sq.m

Following facilities are provided in the Hostel

  • Uninterrupted power supply , separate Generator for Students
  • Medical facility available with free medicines provided to sick students
  • Mineral water provided to all the rooms
  • Library facilities available after the class hours
  • WiFI facility available for 24 hours
  • Modern dining hall serving nutritions and hygienic vegiterian food
  • Stationary store
  • A large modern kitchen with oven and solar steam cooking plant
  • Fully equipped 3 share rooms with attached bathroom

2. LIBRARY FACILITIES

The College library is located in a spacious new building of 468.88 Sq.The library is open to students and staff members from 8.30 a.m. to 6.30 p.m. on all working days.

The library has a rich collection of around 23584 volumes of text books, reference books, national and international journals in all branches of Engineering, Science, Technology, Management and General Studies, and the collection is ever increasing.

The Digital Library provides online resources for students interested in Computer Assisted Learning.

5. CANTEEN

There are two major canteens which offer multi-cuisine food for both resident students and day scholars.
